This is an open source Point of Sale system. Feather is being designed as a project
between

Zachary Hartman
Trevor Huffman
Josh Collins

Feather is intended to be "light as a feather" and storable as a single unit.
Feather can be stored, for instance, on a single computer, and transferred to a
flash drive(s) for portability with an onboard Database (SQLLite). It is
simplistic and is not intended for major company solutions.


Target Audience are small business owners and online shopkeeping solutions.
